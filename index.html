The key to a healthy diet is to eat the right amount of calories for how active you are so you balance the energy you consume withe energy you use. If you eat or drink more than your body needs, you'll put on weight because the energy you do not use is stored as fat.

You should also eat a wide range of foods to make sure you're getting a balanced diet and your body is receiving al the nutrients it needs.
